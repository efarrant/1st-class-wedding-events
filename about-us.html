We are a family run business with over 17 years of event experience, specialising in weddings but able to help with all celebrations. 1st Class Wedding Events started off very small, just doing balloons. We've come such a long way since then, dressed hundreds of venues, and blossomed into the business we are today. We are now proud to be the recommended supplier at jaw-dropping venues such as Knebworth House & Barns, Needham House Hotel, and Cambridge Belfry. You'll also find us on the suppliers list for Hitchin Priory, Bassmead Manor Barns and Wyboston Lakes. We can supply a wide range of decorations and props to ensure we can bring your visions to life. From weddings, birthdays, proms, corporate events, and anniversary parties, we will help you find the perfect decorations to suit all budgets.

Our team has expanded over the years and between us we have a wealth of knowledge. We know how to make your event one to remember. Contact us to discuss your exciting plans, or book a free of charge, no obligation meeting.
